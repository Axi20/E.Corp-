About the company

Evil Corp is one of the largest multi-national conglomerates in the world. Among our enterprises, our manufacture computers, phones, and tablets, and maintain a banking and consumer credit division. The company owns 70% of the global consumer credit industry, which is why fsociety has targeted them.

In 1986, Lester Moore was made CEO. Under him, the company became the world's largest employer in 1990, Bank of E became the world's largest consumer bank in 1992, and E Corp opened manufacturing plants in China, Japan, Singapore, and South Korea in 1993.
